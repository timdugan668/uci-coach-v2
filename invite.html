Features
Everything you need to hit your goal

VAMIQ brings together your Strava data, your training goals and AI coaching into one simple app.

🔴
Strava sync
Connects to your Strava account and analyses your recent rides to understand your current fitness.
📊
Dynamic dashboard
Live fitness metrics — estimated FTP, W/kg, best long ride, and goal readiness bars updated from your real ride data.
📅
Personalised weekly plan
A 7-day training plan with specific HR and power targets for each session, adapted to your goal and available hours.
⚡
Training zones
6-zone power and HR model calculated from your FTP. Know exactly how hard to go in every session.
🤖
AI coach chat
Ask your coach anything — pacing strategy, nutrition, recovery, how to peak for race day. Powered by Claude AI.
🎯
Goal tracking
Set your target event and date. VAMIQ counts down and adapts your plan as race day approaches.

Security & Privacy
Your data is safe

VAMIQ is built with security first. Your ride data and personal information are private and protected.

🔒
Encrypted passwords
Passwords are hashed with bcrypt and never stored in plain text.
🛡️
Private by design
Row Level Security ensures you can only ever see your own data.
🔑
Secure API keys
All API keys live on secure servers — never exposed in the browser.
🚦
Strava read-only
VAMIQ only reads your Strava data. We can never modify or post to your account.
🌏
Hosted in Singapore
Data stored on Supabase AWS Singapore — close to Australian users, fast and reliable.
🔐
HTTPS everywhere
All data transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.
